Classic Chicken Caesar Salad

A Caesar salad (also spelled Cesar and Cesare) is a green salad of romaine lettuce and croutons dressed with lemon juice (or lime juice), olive oil, egg, Worcestershire sauce, anchovies, garlic, Dijon mustard, Parmesan cheese, and black pepper. In its original form, this salad was prepared and served tableside.

Ingredients

  • cups tightly packed chopped romaine lettuce
  • 1 pound cooked boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into strips
  • ½ cup CRACKER BARREL Finely Shredded 100% Parmesan Cheese
  • ½ cup seasoned croutons
  • ¼ cup KRAFT Creamy Caesar Dressing

Steps

  1. Cover 4 plates with lettuce.
  2. Top with all remaining ingredients except dressing.
  3. Drizzle with dressing just before serving.
